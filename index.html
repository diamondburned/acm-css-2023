About

acmCSUF

We are a student chapter for Association for Computing Machinery based in California State University, Fullerton. We strive to bring passionate Titans together into a community that promotes innovation, collaboration, creativity, and inclusivity within tech.

What is ACM?

ACM is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, uniting computer scientists and information technologists from around the world. ACM provides the computing field's premier Digital Library and serves its members and the computing profession with leading-edge publications, conferences, and career resources.

Your journey into tech starts here

We aim to build a foundation for future industry leaders by developing their technical, professional, and social skills. We facilitate these by offering various events, programs, and initiatives that have been proven to help students start up their careers on the right foot.
